Welcome to Bata Book Library

    Welcome to our online library where you can borrow books from the comfort of your own home. We offer a wide variety of books across multiple genres, including fiction, non-fiction, biographies, and more. Our website is easy to navigate, allowing you to search for books by author, title, or keyword.

    Get Started

    Find your favourite book

    Once you find the book you want, simply check it out and start reading. You can borrow up to three books at a time, and the borrowing period is two weeks. Don't worry about late fees, as we automatically return the books for you.
